Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our products, services, shipping, returns, and more.

Products & Orders

How do I know if a watch is in stock?

All watches displayed on our website are typically in stock and ready to ship. If a watch is out of stock, it will be clearly marked as "Out of Stock" or "Pre-order" on the product page. You can also check the estimated shipping time on each product page for more accurate information.

Can I customize my watch?

Yes, we offer customization options for select watch models. Customization options may include:

  • Engraving services for case backs
  • Alternative strap/bracelet options
  • Custom dial colors (for specific collections)

Please note that customization will extend the delivery time by 3-5 business days and may incur additional charges. For detailed information about customization options for specific watches, please contact our customer service team.

How do I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with tracking information. You can also track your order by:

  1. Logging into your account and checking the "Order History" section
  2. Using the tracking number provided in your shipping confirmation email on our website or directly on the courier's website
  3. Contacting our customer service team with your order number
What's included with my watch purchase?

Every watch purchase includes:

  • The watch with original packaging
  • Manufacturer's warranty card (where applicable)
  • Instruction manual
  • Timeless Elegance authenticity certificate
  • Premium gift box
  • Cleaning cloth

Some luxury watch brands may include additional accessories specific to their brand.

Shipping & Delivery

How long will it take to receive my order?

Delivery timeframes depend on your location and shipping method selected:

  • Standard Shipping (Free on orders over $200): 3-5 business days
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days
  • International Shipping: 5-10 business days

Please note that these are estimated delivery times after your order has been processed and shipped. Order processing typically takes 1-2 business days.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary based on location. Import duties, taxes, and customs fees may apply depending on your country's regulations and are the responsibility of the customer.

To check shipping availability and costs for your specific location, please add items to your cart and enter your address at checkout, or contact our customer service team.

Is my shipment insured?

Yes, all shipments are fully insured against loss or damage during transit at no additional cost to you. In the rare event that your package is lost or damaged during shipping, we will either replace the item or issue a full refund.

If you receive a damaged package, please refuse delivery if possible or contact our customer service team within 24 hours with photos of the damaged packaging and products.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn watches in their original condition with all tags and protective coverings intact. To be eligible for a return, your item must be in the same condition that you received it, unworn or unused, with all original tags and packaging.

To initiate a return, please:

  1. Log into your account and navigate to "Order History"
  2. Select the order containing the item you wish to return
  3. Follow the prompts to generate a return label
  4. Pack the item securely in its original packaging
  5. Attach the return label and ship the package

Please note that customized watches cannot be returned unless defective.

How long does it take to process a refund?

Once we receive your returned item, our team will inspect it to ensure it meets our return criteria. After approval, your refund will be processed within 3-5 business days. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Card: 5-10 business days
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days
  • Store Credit: Immediate

You will receive an email notification once your refund has been processed.

Can I exchange my watch for a different model or size?

Yes, exchanges are available within 30 days of purchase. To request an exchange:

  1. Contact our customer service team with your order number and exchange request
  2. Return your unworn watch in its original packaging with all tags attached
  3. Once we receive your return, we'll process the exchange

If the new item costs more than your original purchase, you will need to pay the difference. If it costs less, we'll refund the difference to your original payment method.

Please note that exchange items will be shipped only after we receive and inspect your original return.

Watch Care

How should I clean my watch?

For regular cleaning:

  • Wipe the watch case and bracelet gently with a soft, lint-free cloth to remove dust and oils
  • For water-resistant watches, you can use a slightly damp cloth with mild soap, then wipe with a dry cloth
  • Clean crevices with a soft toothbrush
  • Leather straps should be wiped with a dry cloth only

Avoid:

  • Submerging non-water resistant watches
  • Using chemicals, solvents, or ultrasonic cleaners
  • Exposing leather straps to water

We recommend professional cleaning every 2-3 years, which can be arranged through our service center.

How often should I service my mechanical watch?

Mechanical watches should be serviced every 3-5 years to ensure optimal performance and longevity. During a service, the movement is disassembled, cleaned, lubricated, and reassembled. This helps prevent wear and tear on the components and maintains accuracy.

Signs that your watch might need servicing include:

  • Decreased accuracy (gaining or losing more time than usual)
  • The power reserve is shorter than specified
  • Unusual noises coming from the movement
  • Crown or buttons becoming difficult to operate

Our service center offers complete servicing for all watches sold by Timeless Elegance. Please contact customer service to arrange a service appointment.

Is my watch water-resistant?

Water resistance varies by watch model and is indicated on the case back or in the product specifications. Here's what different water resistance ratings mean:

  • 30m (3 ATM): Resistant to splashes and brief immersion in water, but not suitable for swimming or showering
  • 50m (5 ATM): Suitable for swimming but not for diving or snorkeling
  • 100m (10 ATM): Suitable for recreational swimming and snorkeling
  • 200m+ (20+ ATM): Suitable for high-impact water sports and recreational diving

Important notes on water resistance:

  • Water resistance is not permanent and should be tested annually
  • Always ensure the crown is fully pushed in or screwed down before water exposure
  • Avoid operating the crown or pushers while the watch is wet
  • Water resistance can be compromised by temperature changes, impacts, or aging gaskets

Warranty

What warranty comes with my watch?

All watches purchased from Timeless Elegance come with:

  • The original manufacturer's warranty (typically 2-5 years depending on the brand)
  • An additional 1-year Timeless Elegance warranty that begins after the manufacturer's warranty expires

Our warranty covers manufacturing defects and mechanical failures under normal use. The warranty does not cover:

  • Batteries (except for manufacturing defects)
  • Straps and bracelets (normal wear and tear)
  • Glass/crystal (unless due to manufacturing defects)
  • Damage from accidents, misuse, or unauthorized repairs
  • Normal wear and tear

To maintain your warranty, we recommend servicing your watch only through authorized service centers.

How do I make a warranty claim?

To initiate a warranty claim, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team with your order number and a description of the issue
  2. Our team will provide you with a return authorization and shipping instructions
  3. Send your watch with a copy of your warranty card/proof of purchase
  4. Our technicians will assess the watch and determine if the issue is covered under warranty
  5. If covered, we will repair or replace your watch at no cost
  6. You will be notified when your watch is ready to be returned to you

Warranty processing typically takes 2-4 weeks depending on the complexity of the repair and the availability of parts.

Can I purchase an extended warranty?

Yes, we offer extended warranty options for most watches. Our extended warranty program provides additional coverage beyond the standard manufacturer's warranty and our complimentary 1-year warranty.

Extended warranty options include:

  • Premium Care (2 years): $99 - Covers all mechanical failures, one free maintenance service
  • Elite Care (3 years): $149 - Covers all mechanical failures, one free maintenance service, one free strap replacement
  • Lifetime Care: $299 - Lifetime coverage for mechanical failures, one free maintenance service every two years, one free strap replacement annually

Extended warranties must be purchased within 30 days of your watch purchase. Please contact our customer service team for more details.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we take payment security very seriously. Our website uses the latest encryption technology (SSL/TLS) to protect your personal and payment information. We are PCI DSS compliant, which means we follow all security standards required by the payment card industry.

Key security features include:

  • We never store complete credit card information on our servers
  • All payment processing is handled by trusted, industry-leading payment processors
  • Multiple layers of security including fraud detection systems
  • Regular security audits and testing

If you have concerns about payment security, you can always choose to use PayPal or other alternative payment methods that don't require entering your credit card details directly on our website.
