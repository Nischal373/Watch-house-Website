Our Brand Story

A Heritage of Excellence

Founded in 1985 by master watchmaker Tsuki, Tsuki's Elegance began as a small workshop dedicated to the restoration of vintage timepieces. What started as a passion project quickly evolved into a respected name in the watchmaking industry.

For over three decades, we have been crafting timepieces that combine traditional watchmaking techniques with modern innovation. Our journey has been defined by an unwavering commitment to precision, quality, and timeless design.

Through the years, we have expanded from a single workshop to an international brand with boutiques in major cities across the globe. Despite our growth, we remain true to our founding principles: creating watches of exceptional quality that stand the test of time.

Today, under the leadership of Caroline Chrono, Thomas's daughter, we continue to push the boundaries of watchmaking while honoring the techniques and attention to detail that have made our timepieces cherished heirlooms for generations.

Our Mission

At Tsuki's Elegance, our mission is to create timepieces that transcend functionality to become expressions of personal style and achievement. We believe that a watch is more than an instrument to tell time— it is a companion on life's journey, marking moments and milestones with quiet elegance.

We strive to combine the art of traditional watchmaking with innovative design and technology, creating pieces that are both timeless and contemporary. Through meticulous attention to detail and uncompromising quality standards, we aim to create watches that can be passed down through generations as symbols of enduring craftsmanship.

Beyond creating exceptional timepieces, we are committed to ethical practices, responsible sourcing, and environmental stewardship. We believe that luxury should never come at the expense of our planet or its people.

The Art of Craftsmanship

Every Chrono Elegance timepiece represents countless hours of skilled craftsmanship, combining centuries-old techniques with cutting-edge technology. Our watches are created by master artisans who have dedicated their lives to perfecting their craft.

Precision Engineering

Our movements are assembled by hand, with each component inspected for perfect alignment and functionality. A single watch movement consists of up to 300 parts, each working in perfect harmony.

Material Excellence

We source only the finest materials for our timepieces, from surgical-grade stainless steel to sapphire crystal and genuine leather. Each material is chosen for its beauty, durability, and performance.

Rigorous Testing

Every Tsuki's Elegance watch undergoes extensive testing for water resistance, shock absorption, and accuracy before receiving final approval. Only watches that meet our exacting standards bear our name.
